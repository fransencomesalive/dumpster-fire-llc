CloseSave feedback

Reduced motion: the rotate becomes a cross-fade, no rotation. Keyboard: trigger, chips, Something-else checkbox, note input, Close, and Save are all real controls. On flip, focus moves to the feedback heading; on Close or a successful Save it returns to the trigger. Only the visible face is in the tab order (hidden face is inert). Escape / backdrop on the feedback face flip back to the front, they do not close the whole wizard.
