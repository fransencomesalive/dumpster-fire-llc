Onboarding · Card interior hierarchy · [+] add · saved states

The interior spec every onboarding section card follows (Randall, 2026-07-10). Three rules. One head row: title + teal [+] beside it (the oversized "Add ____" buttons are retired), active-track chip on the right. One intro style: the "You're populating…" line and the card's purpose line share a single font, size, and leading — no more mixed families. Two field states: saved copy renders as plain text with a pencil edit button; the pencil flips it back to the prefilled input with a small Save. Entry-style sections (Work Examples, Skills) collapse each saved entry to a title-only row — see their own cards.
